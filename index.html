About me

I am a consulting software engineer based in Auckland, New Zealand. I received my Ph.D. in Operations Research from the University of Auckland in 2019, advised by Andy Philpott, Andrew Mason, and Anthony Downward.

Projects I am involved in
  1. JuMP: an open-source modeling language for mathematical optimization
  2. dairyanalytics.co.nz: a project forecasting the price of milk in New Zealand
  3. SDDP.jl: a generic open-source solver for sequential decision problems
